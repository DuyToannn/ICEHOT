Bloody Mary

Ingredients

  • 1½ Parts 45ml 1½ oz Absolut Vodka
  • 5 Parts 150 ml 5 oz Tomato Juice
  • 4 Dashes 4Dashes 4 Dashes Hot Sauce
  • ⅓ Part 10 ml ⅓ oz Worcestershire Sauce
  • ⅓ Part 10 ml ⅓ oz Lemon Juice
  • 1 Pinch 1 Pinch 1 Pinch Ground Black Pepper
  • 1 Pinch 1 Pinch 1 Pinch Garlic Salt
  • 1 Wedge 1 Wedge 1 Wedge Lemon
  • 1 Stick 1 Stick 1 Stick Celery

How to make

Fill a mixing glass with ice cubes. Add all ingredients. Stir and pour into a highball glass. Garnish with a lemon wedge and a celery stick.

Story about Bloody Mary

• If you’re making one for brunch, then don’t make your Bloody Mary too strong.

• Legend says that the first Bloody Mary was actually made with gin rather than vodka (which we today know as a Red Snapper).Gin was then substituted with vodka and the drink became incredibly popular in the US!.

• Substitute the tomato juice with Clamato juice and all of a sudden you have a Bloody Caesar instead (in hand)!
