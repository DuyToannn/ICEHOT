Grapefruit Cooler

Absolut Grapefruit, White Wine, Lemon Juice, Agave Nectar, Grape

  • Ice Cubes
  • 1½ Parts Absolut Grapefruit
  • 2⅔ Parts White Wine
  • 6 Whole Grapes
Xem thêm

How to make

Muddle grapes in a shaker. Add lemon juice, agave nectar, white wine, Absolut Grapefruit and ice cubes. Shake and strain into a highball glass. Garnish with grapes.
